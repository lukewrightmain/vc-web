WHAT IS SUMMIT COLLEGE OF MINISTRY?

Summit is an institution of higher education committed to providing learning opportunities to those who desire to reach the “summit of their potential” in serving Jesus Christ. summit is an inter-denominational, non-profit Christian school located in Waupaca, Wisconsin.

WHAT IS SUMMIT COLLEGE OF MINISTRY'S PURPOSE?

To serve those within the body of Christ who are seeking to enter Christian service, currently in part-time Christian service and desire improved effectiveness, and who desire Bible knowledge for the establishment and maintenance of an exemplary Christian lifestyle.

WHAT IS THE COST OF SUMMIT COLLEGE OF MINISTRY?

Summit College - school of lay ministry is a two-year program. there is a one-time registration fee of $40. There are three terms per year: fall term, winter term and spring term. each term is $75. You may earn a two-year certificate upon completion of the requirements.

WHO CAN ATTEND SUMMIT COLLEGE OF MINISTRY?

Anyone looking to develop their leadership abilities and strengthen their relationship with God is welcome to attend.
